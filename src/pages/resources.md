This page collects the **primary and authoritative sources** we cite across StudyAU. When policy, pricing, or admissions rules shift, these pages are **the first place the change is published**. Bookmark this list — every serious question about studying in Australia should be answered against one of these sources rather than a forum post or agent brochure.

> If you find a broken link or have a better canonical source to suggest, email `hello@studyau.au` and we will update within 48 hours.

## Australian Government — visa, immigration, evidence of funds

- [Department of Home Affairs](https://immi.homeaffairs.gov.au) — the master portal for all Australian visas.
- [Student visa (subclass 500)](https://immi.homeaffairs.gov.au/visas/getting-a-visa/visa-listing/student-500) — official rules, evidence, conditions.
- [Temporary Graduate visa (subclass 485)](https://immi.homeaffairs.gov.au/visas/getting-a-visa/visa-listing/temporary-graduate-485) — Post-Higher Education Work and Post-Vocational Education Work streams.
- [Visa Pricing Estimator](https://immi.homeaffairs.gov.au/visas/getting-a-visa/visa-pricing-estimator) — current application charges.
- [ImmiAccount](https://online.immi.gov.au/lusc/login) — the portal where you actually lodge applications.
- [Financial capacity — Student visa](https://immi.homeaffairs.gov.au/visas/getting-a-visa/visa-listing/student-500/financial-capacity) — current evidence-of-funds thresholds.
- [Genuine Student requirement](https://immi.homeaffairs.gov.au/visas/getting-a-visa/visa-listing/student-500/genuine-student-requirement) — the test that replaced GTE on 23 March 2024.
- [Visa conditions — 8202, 8105, 8501, others](https://immi.homeaffairs.gov.au/visas/already-have-a-visa/check-visa-details-and-conditions/see-your-visa-conditions) — condition codes and what each one means.
- [Check visa details online (VEVO)](https://immi.homeaffairs.gov.au/visas/already-have-a-visa/check-visa-details-and-conditions) — confirm visa status.

## Australian Government — education, quality, provider registers

- [Department of Education](https://www.education.gov.au) — Australian Government policy for higher education and VET.
- [Study Australia](https://www.studyaustralia.gov.au) — the Australian Government's official site for international students (costs, course search, FAQs).
- [CRICOS — Commonwealth Register of Institutions and Courses for Overseas Students](https://cricos.education.gov.au) — the only definitive list of providers and courses that can enrol international students.
- [TEQSA — Tertiary Education Quality and Standards Agency](https://www.teqsa.gov.au) — regulator for all Australian higher education providers.
- [National Register (training.gov.au)](https://training.gov.au) — the register of RTOs (VET providers) and accredited courses.
- [ASQA — Australian Skills Quality Authority](https://www.asqa.gov.au) — regulator for VET providers.
- [Provider Register & course search](https://cricos.education.gov.au/Institution/InstitutionSearch.aspx) — check a provider is CRICOS-registered before you pay a deposit.
- [ESOS Framework](https://www.education.gov.au/esos-framework) — the law that protects international students in Australia.

## English-language tests

- [IELTS](https://www.ielts.org) — the most widely accepted test for Australian admissions and visas.
- [IELTS for UKVI / Australian visa test centres](https://www.ielts.org/book-a-test) — find a test centre.
- [PTE Academic](https://pearsonpte.com) — accepted by almost all Australian universities and for visas.
- [TOEFL iBT](https://www.ets.org/toefl) — accepted by most universities.
- [Cambridge English](https://www.cambridgeenglish.org) — C1 Advanced / C2 Proficiency widely accepted.
- [OET — Occupational English Test](https://oet.com) — for healthcare-field study and registration.

## Health cover (OSHC)

Overseas Student Health Cover is mandatory for subclass 500 visa holders. These are the APRA-regulated OSHC providers:

- [Bupa OSHC](https://www.bupa.com.au/health-insurance/cover/oshc)
- [Medibank OSHC](https://www.medibank.com.au/overseas-health-insurance/oshc/)
- [Allianz Care Australia (OSHC)](https://www.allianzcare.com.au/en/students.html)
- [nib OSHC](https://www.nib.com.au/overseas-students)
- [ahm OSHC](https://ahm.com.au/health-insurance/oshc)

Government overview: [Study Australia — health cover](https://www.studyaustralia.gov.au/english/live/health-and-safety/health-insurance).

## Scholarships

- [Australia Awards Scholarships](https://www.dfat.gov.au/people-to-people/australia-awards) — Australian Government scholarships for citizens of partner countries.
- [Destination Australia](https://www.education.gov.au/destination-australia) — scholarships to study in regional Australia.
- [Endeavour Leadership Program](https://www.dfat.gov.au/people-to-people/endeavour-leadership-program) — government-sponsored mobility.
- [Research Training Program (RTP)](https://www.education.gov.au/research-block-grants/research-training-program) — domestic PhD funding; some universities extend RTP stipends to international HDR candidates.
- University-specific international scholarships — check each institution's "International scholarships" page.
